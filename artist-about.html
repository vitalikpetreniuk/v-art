Roman Minin was born in 1981 in Dimitrov (now Myrnograd), Donetsk region. He graduated from the Kharkiv Academy of Design and Arts in 2008 at the Department of Monumental Painting. In 2009 he was awarded the medal "Talent and Vocation" of the World Alliance "Peacemaker". Participant of international art projects and exhibitions, organizer of street art festivals. He founded the directions of SMS art and TRANSMONUMENTALISM. Actively uses virtual and augmented reality in his creative activity. Co-director of the non-profit organization Art Embassy and startup Transmonument. Creates an anthology of mining life.
